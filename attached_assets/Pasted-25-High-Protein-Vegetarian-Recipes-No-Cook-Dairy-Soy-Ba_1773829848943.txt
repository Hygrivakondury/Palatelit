25 High-Protein Vegetarian Recipes (No-Cook)
Dairy & Soy-Based

Cottage Cheese & Hemp Bowl: 1 cup cottage cheese with 3 tbsp hemp hearts and cucumber slices.

Greek Yogurt Whey Mix: 1 cup non-fat Greek yogurt stirred with half a scoop of vanilla whey protein and walnuts.

Savory Tofu Toast: Sliced extra-firm tofu (raw/pressed) on high-protein bread with nutritional yeast and avocado.

Peanut Butter Greek Yogurt Dip: Mix Greek yogurt with 1 tbsp peanut butter; dip apple slices or celery.

Tofu "Egg" Salad: Crumbled tofu mixed with Greek yogurt (instead of mayo), mustard, and turmeric.

Ricotta & Almond Plate: Ricotta cheese topped with slivered almonds and a drizzle of honey.

Paneer Salad (Raw): Cubed fresh paneer tossed with chaat masala, lemon, and raw sprouts.

Legumes & Seeds

Chickpea "Tuna" Salad: Smashed chickpeas, Greek yogurt, capers, and seaweed flakes on a wrap.

Lentil & Feta Bowl: Pre-steamed canned lentils (rinsed) with crumbled feta and olives.

Three-Bean Salad: Kidney beans, black beans, and chickpeas with a lime-cumin dressing.

Edamame & Hummus Wrap: Shelled edamame (thawed) with a thick layer of hummus in a high-protein tortilla.

White Bean & Pesto Mash: Canned cannellini beans mashed with store-bought pesto and pumpkin seeds.

Chia Seed Protein Pudding: Chia seeds soaked in soy milk with a scoop of plant-based protein powder.

Pumpkin Seed Trail Mix: A mix of dry-roasted soy nuts, pumpkin seeds, and almonds (15-20g protein per cup).

Grains & Smoothies

Overnight Oats with Soy: Oats soaked in soy milk (higher protein than almond) with almond butter and chia.

Chocolate Banana Protein Shake: Chocolate whey/plant protein, soy milk, and 1 tbsp peanut butter.

Berry Green Smoothie: Spinach, Greek yogurt, blueberries, and 3 tbsp hemp seeds.

Coffee Protein Frappe: Cold brew coffee, milk/soy milk, and vanilla protein powder blended with ice.

Savory Oat Bran: Cold-soaked oat bran mixed with cottage cheese and black pepper.

Quinoa Salad (Pre-cooked): Use pre-cooked vacuum-sealed quinoa with tomatoes, cucumber, and lemon.

Seitan Deli Sandwich: Pre-packaged seitan slices (no-cook) on high-protein bread with mustard.

Tempeh Strips & Guacamole: Pre-steamed tempeh (ready to eat) dipped in spicy guacamole.

Nutty Protein Balls: Raw oats, peanut butter, honey, and protein powder rolled into bites.

Almond Butter & Hemp Toast: Sprouted grain bread with almond butter and a heavy sprinkle of hemp hearts.

Skyr Fruit Bowl: Icelandic Skyr (higher protein than Greek yogurt) with flaxseeds and berries.
